    Holiday Social 2015

    Celebrate the year with your fellow hackers over food and drink!

    Please join us — a bunch of programmers, nerds, geeks, hackers — to celebrate the year with food, drink, and friends both old and new. Saturday, December 5th, 7PM, at Launch Fishers's New Location! North East side of Indianapolis, we will have the fifth annual Indy Hackers' Holiday Social. We welcome everyone -- Pythonistas, Javanese, COBOLians, Scalanites, PHPers, Rubyists, .NET nuts, Perlsians.... We might even have some hardcore functional programming zealots giving in to mutable state for a few hours. The best part? It is completely free.

  • What's Happening?

    FOODS. DRINKS. PRIZES. Last year was great, so this year is going to be approximately the same because why would you change something great?

    To make this all happen, we need sponsors! Have a job opening for a developer? Want more developers to know of your company? Just want to support the local developer community? Ask for sponsor info today! Absolutely all sponsor money goes toward the event.

    • Food

      Enjoy several options from 10-01 Food and Drink! Mini-cheeseburgers, fried chicken strips, hummus, veggie and fruit trays! Brought to you by Your Company Name could be here.

    • Drink

      For those of age, a variety of beer from Upland, and wine, as well. (Don't worry if you don't drink! There will also be soft drinks.) All of this is brought to you by our sponsor Your Company Name could be here!

    • Games

      Let's play together! We might not all be gamers regularly, but playing Super Mario Kart against friends is almost always fun. What about some NBA Jam (original!)? BOOM SHAKALAKA! How about some board games? Among others, we will have a copy of Kickstarter's best selling board game, Robot Turtles. (Don't worry, there's a set of rules for big people.)
